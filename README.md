# 🤖 PropCall AI — The Conversational Voice Engine for Modern Real Estate

PropCall AI is a next-generation SaaS platform that brings automated, human-like voice intelligence to the real estate sector. Designed to replace cold pipelines and friction-heavy web forms, PropCall AI deploys 24/7 autonomous voice agents trained to qualify buyer leads, schedule property tours, collect booking deposits, and manage property portfolios—instantly, seamlessly, and without human intervention.

---

## 🌟 The Vision

In the fast-moving real estate market, responsiveness is the difference between a closed deal and a lost lead. PropCall AI acts as a digital front-office that never sleeps. When a prospective buyer calls at midnight about a premium listing, PropCall AI answers on the first ring, engages in natural conversation, qualifies their financial readiness, checks agent availability, and books an open house appointment.

---

## 🚀 Core Capabilities

### ⚡ Sub-100ms Conversational Latency
Built on top of leading real-time audio streaming models, PropCall AI achieves ultra-low response latency. Voice agents pause dynamically when interrupted, understand conversational context, and respond with natural inflections.

### 📋 Adaptive Qualification Engine
Our agents don't just chat—they qualify. Through structured objectives (Buying, Renting, Valuations), the voice assistant dynamically steers conversations to gather critical data:
* Pre-approval limits & financing methods
* Immediate move-in schedules
* Desired bedrooms/bathrooms and neighborhood matches
* Appointment preferences

### 📊 Real-Time Analytics & Telemetry
A fully visual, interactive web dashboard gives brokerages immediate visibility. Live call feeds, conversion success rates, booking metrics, and complete transcripts are processed and synced automatically.

### 💳 Stripe & CRM Ecosystem Sync
Integrated Stripe workflows allow builders and agencies to manage membership tiers, collect booking fees, or receive escrow holds directly through conversational API links, while auto-syncing lead metadata to your CRM stack.

## 🛠️ Architecture

* **Frontend**: Next.js (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
* **Database & Auth**: Supabase Postgres & SSR Authentication
* **State Management**: Zustand
* **Calling & Voice Layer**: OpenAI Realtime API WebSockets
